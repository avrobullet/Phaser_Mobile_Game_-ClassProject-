Instructions

Welcome to the game. The goal here is to learn about how a change in velocity and acceleration interacts with the motion of an object and use this to overcome the forces of gravity and momentum as it tries to stop you from moving through the level!

The object of the game is to guide the character to the goal. To do this, you will be given 25 seconds worth of the characters velocity to manipulate, so use it wisely! This curve will be drawn on a canvas representing a velocity vs time relationship. Then when you start the level, the character will use these velocity values to move through the level.
